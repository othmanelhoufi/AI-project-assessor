AI Project Assessment

Choose the right AI tool for the job, based on facts, not hype.

This 5-minute assessment provides an expert recommendation on the best path forward for your AI project. You will receive:

  • Objective Technology Profile: Clear guidance on whether to use Classical ML, Generative AI, RAG, or other solutions.
  • Resource & Timeline Estimates: Realistic estimates for project duration and the team expertise required.
  • Risk & Gap Analysis: Highlights potential risks and identifies where more information is critically needed.
